Keep track of your Stalk Market stock

Log Turnip prices on your island, follow the Turnip trends to predict when the highest prices will happen and calculate the profit you make.

Stalk Market Guide

  • Every Sunday morning, Daisy Mae will appear on your island and sell you Turnips for a random price. Stock up and make a hefty profit!
  • Timmy and Tommy in Nook’s Cranny will buy Turnips every day, except for Sundays.
  • The price changes twice a day: once at midnight, once at noon. TURNIPTRACKER will help you keep track of them!
  • When Daisy Mae arrives in your town again, the Turnips you bought the following week will be spoiled. Make sure you sell them before Sunday!
  • There’s always a risk of taking a big loss. By following the Turnip price trends, you can maximise your chance to make a large profit.

Turnip Price Trends

Decreasing Trend

Usually prices will start out around 80 or 90 Bells, and each price update will drop that by 5 or 10 Bells.

Random Trend

Prices will bounce from 40-200 Bells most of the week. You’re probably better off selling whenever you get above 125 Bells.

Small Spike Trend

You will track a Decreasing Trend followed by three moderate price increases in a row. The fourth price will be dramatically higher.

Large Spike Trend

This pattern will start as the Decreasing Trend followed by three larger increases. You should sell on the 3rd increased price.
